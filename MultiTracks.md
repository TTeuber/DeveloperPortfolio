# MultiTracks Info

My church was not using multitracks when I joined. I saw how it would improve worship at our church plant, so I advocated for adding it and personally implemented it at our Church integrating it with Planning Center and Pro Presenter.

We have now been using multitracks products including Playback, ChartBuilder, and RehearsalMix for over a year now and we love it.

I also volunteer on the tech team, especially the audio team
